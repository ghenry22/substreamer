Substreamer is a music streaming client for Subsonic-compatible servers including Navidrome, Airsonic, Gonic, and more.

Features:
- Stream your entire music library from anywhere
- Background playback with lock screen controls
- Offline mode with automatic music caching
- Browse by album, artist, playlist, or genre
- Search across your entire library
- Scrobbling support
- Customizable themes (light/dark mode)
- High-quality streaming with configurable bitrate
- Share tracks and albums
- Star and rate your favorite music

Substreamer connects to any server that implements the Subsonic API, giving you full control over your music collection.
